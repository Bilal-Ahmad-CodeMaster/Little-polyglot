Smart Parent Academy

At Polyglot Kids, we believe that a child's language success is largely influenced by the parent. You are the one who supports your child in moments of doubt, watches them master new skills and motivates them to continue gaining knowledge. However, we know that parenthood is a huge challenge, which is why we encourage all parents to participate in Smart Parent Academy.

Smart Parent Academy

Smart Parent Academy is a series of activities carried out by Polyglot Kids methodologists and experts in psychology. We run a blog where we discuss topics related to learning English, share ideas for interesting ways to spend time and recommend places worth visiting with a child.

As part of the Smart Parent Academy, we invite you to regular webinars, during which we share knowledge in the field of child psychology, parenting, and creative English learning. You will learn about all the activities carried out in the weekly Newsletter - you can sign up for it by visiting the Polyglot Kids blog.
