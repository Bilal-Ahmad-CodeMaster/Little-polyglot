English classes at Polyglot Kids are an adventure

Every English lesson at Polyglot Kids is an adventure where movement, rhymes, songs and fun lead children straight into the world of free communication (and free exams…). How do we do it?

What does the division into groups look like in Polyglot Kids?

English lessons at Polyglot Kids take place in homogeneous groups, which we create based on both the age and language skills of the students. The duration of classes is longer than in most language schools - from 45 to 90 minutes, depending on whether we are dealing with preschoolers or teenagers. Such lesson planning allows for active contact with the language and effective learning.

Student Development Path

Our student development path allows for effective development of language skills at every stage of learning. The English course at Polyglot Kids provides children and young people with a good start in the future.

More than English

The Polyglot Kids school is full of music, rhymes and movement. We teach English effectively, but not only that. We also value the skills of the future that children will need in their adult lives – including creativity, openness, social skills and critical thinking.
