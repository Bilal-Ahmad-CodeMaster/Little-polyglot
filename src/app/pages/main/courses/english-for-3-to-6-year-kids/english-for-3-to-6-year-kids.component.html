Different learning modes for 3-6 year olds

We know how different the ways of functioning of children are. In order for learning to be effective and enjoyable, it is crucial to properly match the English course to the needs of young children and the organizational possibilities of parents. That is why we have prepared several models of English courses for the youngest children.

Course for 3-5 year olds

  • 1 or 2 in-person lessons per week
  • online materials for consolidating knowledge at home ("Play at Home" parent guide and video lessons)
  • a set of 4 books for reading together at home
  • thematic project

Course for 6-year-olds

  • 1 or 2 in-person lessons per week
  • online materials to consolidate knowledge at home
  • gentle preparation for English classes in the first grade, which will make it easier to start school
  • thematic project

are not just about English!

During English classes for children, we focus not only on learning English, but also on developing non-linguistic skills. It is important for us to develop openness to the world around us in preschoolers and to create opportunities for creative expression. The skills that a child acquires during our classes will also be useful in everyday life and in the school environment.
