Różne formy nauki dla dzieci w wieku 3–6 lat

Rozumiemy, że każde dziecko funkcjonuje inaczej. Aby nauka była zarówno efektywna, jak i przyjemna, kluczowe jest dopasowanie kursu języka angielskiego do potrzeb dziecka i harmonogramu rodziców. Dlatego oferujemy kilka modeli kursów dla najmłodszych.

Kurs dla dzieci w wieku 3–5 lat

  • 1 lub 2 lekcje stacjonarne tygodniowo
  • materiały online wspierające naukę w domu (poradnik dla rodziców „Zabawa w domu” i lekcje wideo)
  • zestaw 4 książek do wspólnego czytania w domu
  • projekt tematyczny

Kurs dla 6-latków

  • 1 lub 2 lekcje stacjonarne w tygodniu
  • Materiały online do utrwalania nauki w domu
  • Delikatne przygotowanie do zajęć z języka angielskiego w pierwszej klasie, ułatwiające rozpoczęcie nauki
  • Projekt tematyczny

Our English courses develop skills
for the future

The comprehensive development of children is our priority. We focus on the gradual deepening of various competencies necessary in the 21st century world.

Openness

We support and develop students’ innate and natural curiosity, build attitudes of respect and tolerance as well as openness to diversity, and make them sensitive to the world and people.

Creative expression

Through games and movement activities, songs, improvisation, theatre elements and art and language projects, we give students the opportunity to express themselves, their opinions and emotions.

English courses for preschoolers

The English language course program for preschoolers aged 3 to 6 is adapted to their age, level, abilities and natural pace of learning. Our effective and friendly method of teaching English makes children want to return to Polyglot Kids lessons to see what our creative teachers have come up with this time!

Polyglot Kids is the first step to a better start in the future. First, but a milestone... With our unique classes, we prove that learning English for 3-, 4-, 5- and 6-year-olds can be great fun!

English classes for 3-6 year olds at Polyglot Kids
are not just about English!

During English classes for children, we focus not only on learning English, but also on developing non-linguistic skills. It is important for us to develop openness to the world around us in preschoolers and to create opportunities for creative expression. The skills that a child acquires during our classes will also be useful in everyday life and in the school environment. Thanks to a properly developed student path, we can be sure that children are constantly developing with us - not only linguistically - from the very beginning of their adventure with English!
