What will your child learn in the
Polyglot Kids course?

Language goals
  • learn to respond to messages and commands in English
  • will practice correct pronunciation and intonation
  • develop the ability to express needs, give commands, requests, describe how they feel, how they look, ask simple questions and provide answers
  • develop reading comprehension skills: words, sentences and short texts
  • develop writing skills in the following areas: single words, sentences, short statements, initially according to patterns
  • will prepare for Cambridge exams at Starters and Movers levels
Non-linguistic goals
  • learn to cooperate in a group and work individually
  • will open up to communication and begin to develop interpersonal skills through working in pairs and groups
  • strengthen your self-esteem and self-confidence through positive experiences and successes in learning English
  • improve your own learning strategy by completing homework assignments and ongoing revision of the material
  • develop creativity and openness to exploring the world
  • will build positive associations related to learning English
  • will experience full support from the leading teacher and the group

Our English courses develop skills
for the future

The comprehensive development of children is our priority. We focus on the gradual deepening of various competencies necessary in the 21st century world.

Focus on growth

We open paths of improvement for students, broaden their horizons and show them that they have an influence on their own development. We encourage them to take on challenges and appreciate their efforts.

Communication skills

We immerse ourselves in English. We allow students to experiment, make mistakes, and learn from them. We encourage students to express their thoughts, emotions, and needs in English.

Creativity

Thanks to the methods used in our courses, we strengthen children's desire to create and look at the world in an original, unconventional, and therefore interesting and exploratory way.

Openness

We support and develop students’ innate and natural curiosity, build attitudes of respect and tolerance as well as openness to diversity, and make them sensitive to the world and people.

Creative expression

Through games and movement activities, songs, improvisation, theatre elements and art and language projects, we give students the opportunity to express themselves, their opinions and emotions.

Developing English for early school age children

The first step to making your child enjoy learning languages!

English for 7-10 year olds – learning and having fun!

English courses for children aged 7–10 at Polyglot Kids are full of humor, movement, and music, which we use in our teaching method. We encourage children to speak English freely in a fun and relaxed atmosphere provided by our creative teachers. We expand students' vocabulary, develop their ability to listen and read their first texts in English. We accustom them to making mistakes so that they do not block the joy of learning.

At this stage, we prepare children for their first Cambridge exams: Pre-A1 Starters, A1 Movers. And we help them pass them relaxed and without stress. English for 7-year-olds and slightly older children at Polyglot Kids is an excellent investment in the future.

English for 7-year-olds at Polyglot Kids is based on an intensive curriculum, which assumes mastering a rich vocabulary and communication skills in English. From the very beginning, 7-year-olds are encouraged to speak to the teacher and to each other in English, thanks to which they subconsciously absorb language structures and understand even complex messages.

Thanks to our 12 superpowers, all classes are interesting and maximally engaging. Thanks to the safe atmosphere, teacher support and exciting activities, students are not afraid to experiment with the language, they quickly become bold and create their own statements, treating English as a natural tool of communication. During our English lessons for 7-, 8-, 9- and 10-year-olds, it is sometimes hard to believe that this is learning! Students have great fun and at the same time gain valuable knowledge.

English classes for children aged 7–10

English classes for 7-, 8-, 9- or 10-year-olds, just like other children, are held in groups that are precisely matched in terms of age and language proficiency level. How do we do it?

To determine the English language level of 8-year-olds and older children, we organize an individual meeting with the school's methodologist before the start of classes. At such a meeting, the child completes a placement test and talks to the methodologist, which allows us to determine the language level of the future student. Thanks to this, children learn in groups with a matched level.

Groups in English classes at Polyglot Kids allow for the appropriate dynamics of work and possible additions to their composition. Each new person can join only after a detailed check of the level of English proficiency. Thanks to this, the list of students is not subject to frequent modifications, which guarantees an even level and ensures progress in learning English for the entire group. We cordially invite all children aged 7, 8, 9 and 10 to English classes!
