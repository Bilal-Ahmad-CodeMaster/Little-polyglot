Our English courses develop skills
for the future

The comprehensive development of children is our priority. We focus on the gradual deepening of various competencies necessary in the 21st century world.

Proactivity

By treating our students as partners, we strengthen their self-confidence and sense of agency. We teach them how to take the initiative and act on a smaller and larger scale.

Autonomy

The oldest students take more and more responsibility for themselves and their decisions. They become independent users of English and conscious citizens of the world.

Ability to learn

We build in our students co-responsibility for the learning process. We equip them with a range of tools that support systematicity and memorization.

Social competences

Through various forms of work, we make people more sensitive to others and diversity. We focus on cooperation, drawing from each other, sharing resources and active listening.

Critical thinking

During classes, we support self-esteem and expressing one’s own opinion, we encourage independent thinking and looking at topics from different perspectives.

Focus on growth

We open paths of improvement for students, broaden their horizons and show them that they have an influence on their own development. We encourage them to take on challenges and appreciate their efforts.

Communication skills

We immerse ourselves in English. We allow students to experiment, make mistakes, and learn from them. We encourage students to express their thoughts, emotions, and needs in English.

Creativity

Thanks to the methods used in our courses, we strengthen children's desire to create and look at the world in an original, unconventional, and therefore interesting and exploratory way.

Openness

We support and develop students’ innate and natural curiosity, build attitudes of respect and tolerance as well as openness to diversity, and make them sensitive to the world and people.

A unique English language school for young people aged 15-19

English for teenagers at Polyglot Kids is an important investment in your teenager's future. Secondary school is a real challenge for teenagers and a time of first serious decisions. Fluent English is their chance for international friendships, academic success, scholarships, and hobbies.

Our program and the 12 Superpowers method help teenagers get used to exam situations and freely express their thoughts in English. Thanks to this, they not only pass key language exams with ease, but also develop the ability to work in a group, be proactive, and think critically.

English for high school students tailored to their needs

Why are English lessons for teenagers at Polyglot Kids so effective? Because we treat our students as partners – we give them the opportunity to express themselves creatively, engage them in class and support them in building autonomy. We know that making mistakes is part of the learning process, so we teach teenagers to be open to their own mistakes and the mistakes of others. Thanks to this, we have a relaxed, friendly atmosphere that teenagers really need.

During English lessons at Polyglot Kids, teenagers aged 15-19 receive ambitious challenges, learn active cooperation in a group, and leadership behavior. Thanks to this, they acquire competences that allow them to take life into their own hands. Solid linguistic and non-linguistic preparation gives Polyglot Kids students a pass to travel, study, and work freely.

English classes in Polyglot Kids for 15-19 year olds

English classes at Polyglot Kids are designed specifically for teenagers aged 15-19. These classes provide a tailored, engaging environment for language learning, encouraging students to actively participate in group discussions and individual assignments. The 12 Superpowers method is fully integrated into the curriculum, allowing students to develop not only their English skills but also their critical thinking and leadership abilities.

We also place a strong emphasis on helping students build confidence and comfort in their language abilities, which prepares them for major exams. Our approach fosters an environment where students feel comfortable making mistakes and growing through them.

For new students, we organize individual leveling meetings with the methodologist to ensure groups are appropriately matched in terms of language level. This helps in creating stable, cohesive groups that contribute to a better learning environment.
