Our English courses develop skills
for the future

The comprehensive development of children is our priority. We focus on the gradual deepening of various competencies necessary in the 21st century world.

Ability to learn

We build in our students co-responsibility for the learning process. We equip them with a range of tools that support systematicity and memorization.

Social competences

Through various forms of work, we make people more sensitive to others and diversity. We focus on cooperation, drawing from each other, sharing resources and active listening.

Critical thinking

During classes, we support self-esteem and expressing one’s own opinion, we encourage independent thinking and looking at topics from different perspectives.

Focus on growth

We open paths of improvement for students, broaden their horizons and show them that they have an influence on their own development. We encourage them to take on challenges and appreciate their efforts.

Communication skills

We immerse ourselves in English. We allow students to experiment, make mistakes, and learn from them. We encourage students to express their thoughts, emotions, and needs in English.

Creativity

Thanks to the methods used in our courses, we strengthen children's desire to create and look at the world in an original, unconventional, and therefore interesting and exploratory way.

Otwartość

We support and develop students’ innate and natural curiosity, build attitudes of respect and tolerance as well as openness to diversity, and make them sensitive to the world and people.

Twórcza ekspresja

Through games and movement activities, songs, improvisation, theatre elements and art and language projects, we give students the opportunity to express themselves, their opinions and emotions.

English language courses for teenagers aged 11-14 – this is what you get in the package

English courses for teenagers aged 11–14 are a continuation of the earlier stages of education. Their intensive program and ambitious materials develop students' ability to communicate fluently in English. In addition, with the involvement of our 12 superpowers, they comprehensively prepare for Cambridge exams at the following levels: Movers, Flyers, Key for Schools, Preliminary for Schools.

We believe that every English course for teenagers is an investment in their future. At this stage of language education, our students successfully pass the eighth-grade exam, and by the end of primary school, they achieve a level of English equivalent to the basic matriculation exam.

Thanks to a well-planned student path, everyone at Polyglot Kids can achieve success, not only in exams!

English for 11-14 year olds and the skills of the future

As our students have discovered, Polyglot Kids English for Teens is more than just learning. Students in this age group increasingly take the lead, work in pairs, groups and small teams. This gives them many opportunities to actively use language with their peers. Working on different challenges, they learn to think critically and express their opinions, as well as listen empathetically and solve problems together. These are essential skills for the future!

English classes in Polyglot Kids for 11-14 year olds

English classes at Polyglot Kids are held in age-matched groups, divided into different levels of advancement. Progress in learning is constantly monitored through tests, quizzes and exams. Thanks to them, we are sure that the Polyglot Kids method is extremely effective.

We make sure that English classes for teenagers at our school are effective, but also comfortable. The size of the groups at Polyglot Kids promotes active communication, ensures appropriate dynamics of work and allows for the implementation of original projects.

For new students, we organize individual leveling meetings with the methodologist. Thanks to them, we can be sure that the groups are well-matched in terms of language level. And the optimal composition of the group means that its core is not subject to constant changes. In this way, the bonds between students are constantly strengthened, and the level of learning is evened out over the years.

We invite students aged 11 to 14 to classes at Polyglot Kids. We also recommend our English courses for children and older teenagers.
