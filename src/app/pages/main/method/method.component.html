Our method is the result of many years of experience in teaching children and young people. We operate in accordance with the growth mindset approach. It assumes a developmental view of the capabilities of the human brain, treating learning as a process. Thanks to it, our students are not afraid of challenges and do not treat mistakes as failures. They willingly take the risk of further development, because they know that a possible stumble does not invalidate their previous achievements. It is only a stage on the way to a new goal.

By teaching English, we simultaneously develop in students the competences of the future, useful in other areas of life, including: creativity, openness, critical thinking and a focus on development.

Polyglot Kids Method – 12 superpowers

At Polyglot Kids, English is a great adventure – appropriately adapted to the age and level of our students.

To make it easier for students to learn English, we use proven techniques for working with children and young people. They are arranged in an original lesson structure that ensures methodological purpose and gives space for spontaneity, joy, and authentic contact between students, the teacher, and the group.

Method of teaching English at different language levels

We ensure our teachers receive ongoing methodological support and opportunities for professional growth.

This is essential because – depending on the age of the students – we use our superpowers in different ways. For the youngest groups, classes are filled with movement, songs, and fun ( Kids  &  Juniors ), while older students focus on active communication and preparation for exams ( Teens  &  Masters ).
