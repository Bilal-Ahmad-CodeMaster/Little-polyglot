Early Stage English Courses

Kids

3–6 years

Rhymes & Play

Rhymes, songs, movement and art are the elements of preschoolers and our way of opening them to the world of English. Classes for children aged 3–6 are conducted in a safe, friendly atmosphere, accompanied by smiles from ear to ear and valuable fun.

Focus: Joyful learning, creative play DETAILS

Juniors

7–10 years

Talk & Create

We encourage early school children to speak. We expand their vocabulary, develop their listening comprehension and reading skills for the first texts in English. We accustom them to making mistakes so that they do not block the joy of learning.

In classes for 7–10 year olds, we prepare children for their first Cambridge exams: Pre-A1 Starters, A1 Movers. And we help them pass them relaxed and without stress.

Exams: Pre-A1 Starters, A1 Movers DETAILS

Teens

11-14 years old

Think & Cooperate

Our teenagers are already chatty – and in English! The topics of classes for children aged 11-14 encourage them to think critically, present their own opinions and solve problems creatively.

At this stage, we prepare for the eighth-grade exam and the subsequent Cambridge exams: A2 Flyers, A2 Key, B1 Preliminary. During the preparations, our students also learn to cooperate and celebrate small and large successes!

Exams: A2 Flyers, A2 Key, B1 Preliminary DETAILS

Masters

15-19 years old

Share & Challenge

Early Stage classes for young people are a forge of free conversations in English and an almost unlimited field for practicing expressing one's opinions in a thoughtful way. We support looking at the world from different perspectives, autonomy and proactivity of students.

At this stage, we prepare young people for the extended English language matriculation exam and Cambridge exams at the following levels: Preliminary for Schools, First for Schools, Advanced. We give them "linguistic wings" and help them fly in the direction of their dreams.

Exams: B2 First, C1 Advanced DETAILS
